#E-Commerce-Business-Intelligence-Using-Python

A Python and Pandas based data analytics project that analyzes e-commerce sales data to generate business insights through exploratory data analysis (EDA) and data visualization.

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an E-Commerce sales dataset using Python to extract meaningful business insights. The analysis includes data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and data visualization to understand sales performance, customer behavior, product categories, and revenue trends.

---
## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top-selling products
- Analyze monthly revenue trends
- Evaluate product category performance
- Study customer purchasing behavior
- Analyze payment method preferences
- Generate business insights for decision-making

## Business Insights
- Books and Toys generated the highest overall revenue.
- Monthly revenue shows noticeable sales variations.
- Credit Card is the most preferred payment method.
- Product price has a stronger impact on revenue than discounts.
- High-performing categories should be prioritized for future marketing campaigns.
- Customer retention strategies can improve long-term revenue.
